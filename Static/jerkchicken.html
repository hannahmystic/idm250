Side of the Road Jerk Chicken App

This is an app design for the Philadelphia based company Side of the Road Jerk Chicken. I worked with 3 others in a team, as well as the manager of the company himself, to synthesize the menu and order process into an app that would be functional, convenient, and entertaining for regular and new customers alike.
In the Flinto software, the prototype is interactive, and the user can scroll through the menu and go through the process of placing an order.
