"Self Portrait in Purple"

My little dog Phineas is my muse. I draw him all the time, and accidentally find elements of him popping up in every animal I draw. These works emerged from a sereies of portraying Phineas every day in different styles. However, they have transcended the exercise and become an entity of their own-- a way to solidify Phineas as the icon he was born to be.

Completed as vector art, and done entirely with a computer mouse, I might add, this simple portrait and pop-art spin have become my favorite of my artwork. I am arranging to put them on t-shirts for my family and friends.
