Cherri Cola

My biggest project for the past year has been my band, Cherri Cola. We are a three-girl punk power-group focused on smashing the patriarchy and snuggling puppies. We do all of our own work including recording, mixing, booking, merchandising, and advertising. On stage, I sing and play lead guitar. But behind the scenes, one of my main roles has been making our website.
